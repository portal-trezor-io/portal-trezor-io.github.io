Starting Up Your Trezor Device

Get started with Trezor.io/start (version 22.9.3) and protect your crypto with ease. Follow simple setup steps to activate your Trezor wallet, ensuring your assets are secure from online threats. Begin your safe crypto management now.

Step-by-Step Guide

    1. Visit Trezor.io/start: Access the setup page.
    2. Connect Trezor Device: Plug in your Trezor wallet.
    3. Install Trezor Bridge: Follow on-screen prompts.
    4. Create a Wallet: Set a secure PIN and backup your recovery seed.
    5. Start Using Trezor: Begin managing your cryptocurrencies securely.
